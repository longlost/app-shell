Use Offline

When this setting is on, the app will keep a copy of your data on this device.

The local copy is used as a fallback when you are offline, or when you have a poor connection.

If this is a public or shared device, we recommend turning this feature off.

Some features may not work offline.

Install

Enjoy a better experience by installing the app to your home screen.

You get a quick launch icon so you can jump right back in easily.

And installed apps launch into a standalone mode which gives you more screen real estate.
